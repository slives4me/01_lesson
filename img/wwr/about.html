"Founded in 2024, Rocky Mountain White Water Rafting has been providing thrilling whitewater rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. Our founder, Steven Robert Lagaisse was passionate about outdoor recreation and saw an opportunity to share his love of rafting with others. With just a few rafts and a small team of guides, Billy began leading trips on some of the most scenic and challenging rivers in the area.
